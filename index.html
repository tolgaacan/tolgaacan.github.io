2021

Ripple Studios Inc.

In 2021, I was working a job as a research assistant at Toronto Metropolitan University, helping with the development of an online video game called Cognigeeks. Throughout this experience, I helped with writing the game's story, facilitating voice lines for each character, and worked with a game developer to adjust and implemenet user interfaces that improved the learnign experience for the audience. The audience that the game was developed for, were STEM students in secondary school.
2022 - 2024

Honing Skills In Graphics Design

During this 2-year time period, I decided to take a break from working in the post-secondary education system, to adjust my focus into developing skills in graphics design, by creating projects using resources such as Premiere Pro, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, DaVinci Resolve, and Blender. My most notable project during this time period, was the nothingcontest YouTube channel, which garnered over 1,500 views and 30 subscribers, changing the way video games are interpreted in modern-day media.
